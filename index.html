FAQ

What is Framo?

Framo is an AI-powered video editing agent that automatically creates compelling short-form video content by deeply understanding your video visually. Just describe what you want in natural language, and Framo does the editing for you.

How does Framo differ from traditional video editors?

Unlike traditional video editors that require manual timeline adjustments, Framo uses advanced Vision-Language Models (VLM) along with its proprietary model trained specifically on video data. This unique combination enables Framo to intelligently identify key moments and highlight-worthy scenes purely based on visual content, significantly streamlining the editing process.

Do I need video editing experience to use Framo?

No experience is necessary! Framo is designed for creators of all skill levels. Simply describe your desired outcome, and the AI handles the rest.

What kinds of videos can Framo handle?

Framo is optimized for short-form videos used on platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and more, making it ideal for creators, marketers, gamers, streamers, and vloggers.

Can I manually adjust edits made by Framo?

Yes! Framo provides an optional "Editor Mode" where you can review, refine, and manually adjust edits while still interacting naturally with the AI assistant.

How fast can Framo produce edited videos?

Framo can process and generate engaging edits in minutes, making it significantly faster than manual video editing and ideal for quick content turnarounds.

Is my video content secure with Framo?

Absolutely. Your videos are processed securely, ensuring complete privacy and confidentiality. You retain full control and ownership of all uploaded content.
